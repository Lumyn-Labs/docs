# Known Issues

This page documents known bugs, compatibility issues, and potential workarounds for Lumyn Labs products.

## Device Config Larger Than 4KB Fails to Apply

**Status**: Planned fix

When applying a configuration over USB, particularly large configurations (>4KB) may fail to apply.

**Workaround**: Copy the configuration file directly to the device's SD card instead of applying over USB.

---

## Device Freezes with Solid Teal LED on Boot

**Status**: Under investigation

In some cases, the device's status LED may turn solid teal and freeze during boot.

**Workaround**: Unplug and replug the device, or press the RST/RESET button to restart. If the issue persists, please email us at [support@lumynlabs.com](mailto:support@lumynlabs.com) with your device configuration file and steps to reproduce.
